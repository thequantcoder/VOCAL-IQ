# VocalIQ — Reseller Guide

You operate a white-labeled sub-platform with your own branding, domain, pricing, and customers.

## Setup
1. **Branding + domain** — set your logo/colors and connect a custom domain (White-label).
2. **Plans** — build your own plans/prices (Plans) with your markup; assign to customers.
3. **Wallet + markup** — set your default markup; the engine bills your customers at retail and
   credits your margin (wholesale → retail is reconciled per period).

## Managing customers
- **Sub-tenants** — provision, suspend, and manage isolated customer workspaces (fully RLS-isolated —
  you never see another reseller's data).
- **Revenue** — your dashboard shows revenue, cost, margin, and top clients per period.

## Support
Your customers see your brand end-to-end; escalate platform issues to the operator via Support.
